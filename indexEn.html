Who is Emre Cihan??

Hello, I'm Emre—an avid enthusiast in technology and innovation. Having completed the compulsory English preparatory program in 2020-2021, I ventured into the world of Electrical and Electronics Engineering.

My academic journey encompasses a broad spectrum, including embedded systems engineering, automation, and ongoing theoretical and simulation-based training in HMI Panel, PLC programming, and more.

I'm committed to continuous learning and currently immersing myself in diverse topics such as:

  • SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition)
  • Communication Systems
  • STM Processors

While my practical experience in PLC programming and HMI panel programming is in the early stages, I am actively engaged in theoretical exploration and simulation exercises.

I believe in the power of adaptability and continuous growth, eagerly applying my evolving knowledge to theoretical scenarios.
